It is also called braised spicy chicken.
It is a chicken dish that is boiled by cutting chicken
into pieces and mixing them with spicy sauce and evenly
with spicy sauce. It is a medium form of soup
with a lot of soup and steam with little soup,
which is closer to braised soup. It is one of the traditional Korean dishes,
and the recipes vary slightly depending on the region.
Chuncheon Dakgalbi is served with marinated
chicken and vegetables in a large, round, iron-frying pan.
Although it was a local dish representing Chuncheon along
with Makguksu, it has now spread throughout the country
and has become a popular dining menu for everyone.
